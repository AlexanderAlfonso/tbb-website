Fitness

Looking to test your fitness and bring it to new heights? Our Fitness class will do just that. Our CrossFit class implements constantly varied, functional movements performed at an appropriately high intensity. Easy to scale and modify for all levels of fitness, weather you are a beginner, weekend warrior or everyday go getter. Our class is perfect for all.

Blueprint

A combination of both a cardio-intensive program (mixture of high-intensity intervals and longer, low-intensity work) as well as bodybuilding and functional fitness movements. You will use a variety of aerobic tools such as row ergs, ski ergs, assault runners, assault bikes, and speed ropes combined with bodyweight movements and very light weights to keep your heart rate elevated for optimal fat loss.

Boxing

Our high intensity cardio based boxing class will not only be fun and a great workout as you hit the bag round for round then hit the floor for a gut crunching AB workout. It will also be just as informative as you will learn the beginning fundamentals to the great sport of Boxing.

Kids

Our kids program is designed for our youth athletes ages 5-17 years old, focusing on developing overall athleticism and proper movement mechanics. They are taught to move well now so that they continue to move well (and prevent injuries) as they continue into adulthood. By incorporating running, jumping, pushing, pulling and other activities kids naturally enjoy doing, we create a fun, play-like group environment where kids thrive.
